The gRATEful App

Over the course of their 30 year musical history, the Grateful Dead played more than 2,300 live shows around the globe. The goal of this app is to find the "best" live versions of any song!

This app is a project for Nashville Software School.

It is built upon the AngularJS framework and uses Firebase in conjunction with Spotify's API.

Instructions

1. Login using your Google account and Spotify account.

2. Search a song. Upvote or downvote it, leave a comment, or add it to your favorites!

3. Enjoy!
